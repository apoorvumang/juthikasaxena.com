Juthika Saxena Short Bio

I’m Juthika Saxena, a finance transformation consultant at PwC, working with organizations to improve finance processes, reporting, controls, and technology-led ways of working. My work sits at the intersection of finance, operations, people, and systems, helping teams move from fragmented, manual processes to cleaner, more insight-driven ways of working.

Beyond consulting, I’m interested in how AI can make finance and business workflows faster, clearer, and more practical. I’m also involved in entrepreneurial ventures including Empire Raas and Everything 3D, which keep me close to customers, brand-building, operations, and real-world execution.

Outside work, I’m a mother and someone who takes fitness seriously. For me, motherhood and workouts are not separate from my professional life. They shape how I think about discipline, energy, ambition, and growth.
